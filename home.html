Sizzling Beef Tapa

Tapsilog is a popular breakfast dish consisting of Filipino beef tapa, fried egg, and garlic fried rice. This dish can also be served in a sizzling plate.

Lunch

Kare-Kare

Kare Kare is a type of Filipino stew (kare derived from "curry") with a rich and thick peanut sauce. It is a popular dish in the Philippines served during special occasions.

Meryenda

Turon

Turon are fried spring rolls filled with ripe saba banana, jackfruit, and brown sugar. Typically served for merienda or dessert, it has the perfect blend of flavor and texture.

About Us

Our website features a vast collection of traditional Filipino dishes such as adobo, pancit, kare-kare, and lechon, complete with detailed instructions and helpful tips to make cooking a breeze. At Sizzling Pinoy, we bring you the best Filipino recipes prepared with love and care, just like how it's done in our Filipino homes. We believe that food is a reflection of culture, tradition, and values, and our passionate team of food enthusiasts works tirelessly to showcase the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines.

At Sizzling Pinoy, we believe that good food brings people together. So, we invite you to join us on this gastronomic journey and discover the flavors of the Philippines.
